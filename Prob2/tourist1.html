Calle Crisologo,
Vigan

Travel back in time as you stroll along the streets of Calle Crisologo in Vigan. This 16th-century town, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, quietly boasts old-world charm and Spanish colonial architecture and is the top attraction in Vigan, Ilocos tours.

The best time to visit for Calle Crisologo excursions is during the first week of May when the town celebrates the Vigan Festival of the Arts.
